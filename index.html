Alexander Wang

Alexander Wang is a prominent American entrepreneur and the co-founder of Scale AI, a company that provides data labeling and model evaluation for AI development. Born in Los Alamos, New Mexico, to physicists, he showed an early talent for computer science. Wang dropped out of MIT at 19 to co-found Scale AI in 2016. Under his leadership, the company quickly became a crucial partner for businesses and governments working with artificial intelligence. His success led him to become the world's youngest self-made billionaire at age 24. He stepped down as CEO of Scale AI in 2025 to join Meta but remains on the company's board.
